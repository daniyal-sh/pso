## Learn patterns first

Organic chemistry becomes manageable when you learn electron movement, functional group behavior, and common reaction families instead of memorizing isolated facts.

## Core map

- Substitution and elimination.
- Addition to alkenes and carbonyls.
- Aromatic substitution.
- Oxidation and reduction.
- Stereochemistry and mechanism reasoning.

## Practice

After each reaction family, solve mechanism prediction and synthesis questions with explanations.
