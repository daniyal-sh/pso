## The first five minutes

Before calculating, identify the system, constraints, conservation laws, and approximations. Long problems often become easier once the structure is visible.

## Working method

- Draw a clean diagram and label symbols.
- Write known relationships before substituting numbers.
- Check units after every major equation.
- Preserve intermediate results so later parts can reuse them.

## After solving

Rewrite the solution in a shorter form. This turns a one-time struggle into reusable technique.
