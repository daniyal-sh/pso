## Start with the exam shape

NSTC preparation rewards breadth, accuracy, and calm timing. Begin by mapping the subjects you want to attempt, then create a weekly schedule that alternates concept review with timed practice.

## Four-week foundation

- Week 1: diagnostic test and syllabus mapping.
- Week 2: core formulas and high-frequency concepts.
- Week 3: topic-wise MCQs and error logs.
- Week 4: mixed mocks, review, and pacing.

## Keep the feedback loop short

Every practice session should end with one clear next action: reread a concept, solve five similar questions, or ask a mentor for the missing idea.
