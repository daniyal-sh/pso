## Camp mindset

Training camps are intense because they compress lectures, problem solving, experiments, peer learning, and selection pressure into a short period. The best campers stay curious and consistent.

## Daily habits

- Review lecture notes the same day.
- Ask precise questions after attempting the problem.
- Sleep enough before practical or long problem sessions.
- Keep a tiny notebook of recurring mistakes.

## Community

Your peers are part of the learning environment. Explain solutions, trade approaches, and build relationships that last beyond selection.
