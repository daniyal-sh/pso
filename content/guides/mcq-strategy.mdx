## MCQs reward decisions

Good MCQ practice is not guessing faster. It is learning when to compute, estimate, eliminate, and move on.

## A simple solve loop

- Read the target quantity before reading every number.
- Estimate the order of magnitude.
- Eliminate impossible units or signs.
- Compute only when the answer choices demand precision.
- Mark questions that need review and return with fresh eyes.

## Review

Track whether mistakes came from concept gaps, algebra slips, misreading, or time pressure. Each category needs a different fix.
