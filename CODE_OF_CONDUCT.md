# Code of Conduct

Pakistan Olympiads should be a respectful, welcoming learning community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Help beginners without condescension.
- Credit sources and contributors.
- Keep discussions focused on learning and community improvement.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of sources.
- Gatekeeping based on school, city, background, or prior access.
- Sharing private information without consent.

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to protect the community.
